Step 1: Create a Steam Account

  1. Create a Steam account and download the game.
  2. Go to the Steam store and search for Kaidro: Clan Battles.
  3. Download the game and wait for it to appear in your library.
  4. Log into the game and create an in-game account.
  5. Remember your username and password as you will need to log into the Kaidro website later on.
Download the game on steam here

Step 2: Create a Ronin Wallet and Mint a Journal (This is Free)

  1. Create an account on the Kaidro website.
  2. Ensure you have a Ronin wallet. There is a direct link to a tutorial on how to create a Ronin wallet below.
  3. Once you have a Ronin wallet, go to Kaidro.com.
  4. Click the "Free Journal Mint" button at the bottom right of your screen.
  5. Follow the prompts and wait, as minting may take some time.
  6. Once completed, congratulations! You are now part of the Kaidro universe.

Step 3: Collect badges and accumulate at least 150 rank points.

  • Collect badges and spin until you get at least 150 points. This will qualify you for some share in $KDR.
  • You will need to claim 1 Kalodium a day and spin that until you accumulate 150 points. It may be challenging, but luck could be on your side.
