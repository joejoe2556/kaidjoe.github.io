Step 1: Create a steam account

Create a steam account and download the game. Go to the steam store look for Kaidro: Clan battles. Download and wait for it to appear in the library. Log into the game. Create an in-game account. Remember your username and password as you will be logging this into the Kaidro website later on.

Download the game on steam here

Step 2: Create a ronin wallet and mint a journal (this is free)

Create an account in the Kaidro website. Now for this step you need to have a ronin wallet. I've placed a direct link to a tutorial how to create a ronin wallet below. Once you have a ronin wallet go to Kaidro.com. Click the "Free journal mint" at the buttom right of your screen. Follow the prompts and wait as minting may take some time. Once that's done you congratulations you are now part of the kaidro universe.

Step 3: Collect badges and accumulate at least 150 rank points.

If you collect badges and spin until you get at least 150 points, you will qualify for some share in $KDR. You will have to claim 1 Kalodium a day and spin that until you get 150 points. It will be a struggle but if you're lucky you just might make it. Another option is to buy NFTs click the link below to find out how.
