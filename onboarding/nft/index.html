IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER

There are 2 games. One game is called Kaidro: Clan Battles and is purely web2. It's a MOBA shooter that lives on steam. NFTs are used purely as skin within this game. The second game lives on the Kaidro.com website and is the web3 aspect of Kaidro. In this web3 game, You combine NFTs to synergize and give points towards a monthly leaderboard. $KDR will be distributed in proportion to the number of points you accumulate in a month and the number of people who participate (very similar to axie's play to rice system). There is also a spin wheel which will give you the chance to earn more points and get ahead, and prevents the game from being too predictable. Other prizes (merch, NFTs, etc) can be won as well. Read about the details of KDR below.

Step 3: Collect badges and plan what NFT you will buy

Once the free journal is minted in the kaidro.com website you can start fulfilling your quests. You will need to fulfill a minimum 14 quests (collect 14 badges) before you can participate in the $KDR distribution. You will also need to combine 3 NFTs of the same clan to synergize and produce points. If you combine the same color theme, and combine a pilot, spirit guardian and spark suit you will get bonuses.

Step 4: Buy RON to be able to buy NFTs

RON is the primary token used to transact in the mavis market place. Determine how much RON you need to buy your NFTs that you want or planned using the calculator. Once you're ready buy RON. Click the first button below to know how and where to buy RON.

Once you have the RON head over to the MAVIS MARKET. I placed a link that will take you directly to the different NFT collections. Once in the mavis market you can use the filter to look for traits that you want your NFTs to have. For synergy, COLOR THEME is the trait that gives a bonus. All 3 NFTs must have the same color theme. The higher the TIER the higher the base points. Refer back to the synergy calculator in step 3 if you need guidance. Once you buy all NFTs, head over to Kaidro.com and set up your synergies in the synergy page

Step 5: Go to kaidro.com to build your synergy

Go to kaidro.com. Go to profile page. Click the synergy tab within the page. Each synergy will cost 1RON/KDR that diminishes 50% for every lock up You have to set your synergy from Sunday 0:00 UTC to Monday 0:00 UTC weekly. Synergy points apply to the leaderboard every Saturday. You will also be given 1 Kalodium for every 5 synergy points you create, which will also be given on Saturday. You can use the Kalodium to spin in the spinwheel and increase your leaderboard rankings. Join can the Kaidro discord community to stay up to date with the latest events in the kaidro community. You can also ask questions there if you feel that any of this is too difficult.
