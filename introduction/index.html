What is Kaidro?

Kaidro the awakening is a one of a kind intellectual property that has three components - a comic, a game and an animated series. All these are meant to take its consumers on a fully immersive experience across an ever-expanding universe. The lore is set on the post-apocalyptic yet beautiful world of T'aljor. It is shrouded in the mists of ancient magic and technological wonders. In this world, Gaia is the deity and there are two races. The Trakarians and K'ailantians. The K'ailantians are revered for their innate connection to the astral realm granting them unparalleled abilities to manipulate the forces of the cosmos. They are like guardians of ancient wisdom and custodians of spiritual balance within T'aljor. In contrast, the Trakarians have dedicated themselves to the pursuit of knowledge and technological innovation. They themselves do not have inherent magical abilities. Trakarians compensate with war machines and mech suits. We know that a lot of conflict arises from the Trakarians driving the K'ailantians out and mining Kalodium the world resource.

There exists a special lineage of K'ailantians known as the Kaidros. It translates literally to source bearer. One who can harness the raw power and energy of the astral realm. They are 10x more powerful than the average K'ailantian. In the comics and animation We follow the story of Ava a young kaidro as she discovers her identity and her powers. It’s a fun story. You can read it now completely on webtoons or in the kaidro.com website. If you are one of the 90s kids who grew up with the old school mechs - Gundam and evangelion, BTX - then this IP is for you.

Who are the developers?

It all started with Peggy Chung and Robert Simons. They hail from the Art Center College of Design, one the best graphic designs schools in the US. Robert continued his work in cinema with director Michael Bay on Transformers. Peggy worked with Guillermo Del Toro on “Pacific Rim”. They realized that there was a demand for top-tier designers in the field so they established Gadgetbot productions in 2011.

Since then they have been engaged in character concept art, Environment concept art Esports weapons concept art, 3D Game assets - characters and weapons, Films production and development, Games development, Animation production and development They’ve worked with big titles in the gaming industry, big triple A games - Call of duty, Apex Legends, Borderlands - and big movie titles as well. These guys are not only professionals in the field of graphic design but they are also professional storytellers and that's what makes me personally bullish about this project.

I think they are the most qualified to create NFTs in this space precisely because they can easily create experiences that people can enjoy. Access to experiences is what gives NFT utility and value. The team's ability to create immersive expereinces is what will keep the economy alive and would prevent people from cashing out the token every time they extract it from the IP. Sustainability is achieved the moment people stop exchanging your token for cash and actually spending it on the IP to immerse further. The game

The game is a PvPvE isometric shooter that is a multiplayer online battle arena. You start as a team of 3 and battle 2 other teams for points. You get points by killing each other and other VOID entities. There is a big boss in the middle and killing him gives significant points. There’s not a lot to say as of now because we are still in the alpha phase. But what I can say is that it is really fun and it's really and competitive. I also like That the game is free to play and is not gated by the NFT. This means you can download it and play even without having an NFT. The NFT acts purely as a skin in-game. It does not give any advantage, extra stats or anything like that.

I personally prefer it to be that way, because nobody likes a pay to win game. When you look at games that have done so well over the past decade it’s the games that are free to play games that are skill-based. I mean look at DOTA, Counter-Strike or Valorant these games are massive with million dollar, community-funded, prize pools, all because these are free-to-play and skill-based. Make a game pay to win and it dies. The game is built on steam. This is a huge step for the web3 community as it opens up an opportunity to onboard more web2 users into the space. Kaidro is leading the way.

It was leaked to us that the game will also have a PVE mode very much like diablo where you just have wholesome fun with friends going on missions together in a casual, non-competitive way. What's unique about it is that the events/activities quests in game will also be part of the lore. It is called clan battles precisely because there are four clans within T’aljor Grimbone, Bubblegum violence Emerald hand, and Free wrist clan. Tokenomics

There are currently 2 tokens. $KDR and $Kalodium. $KDR is the main, tradeable token. It's set to release in September 2024. We know that it will play a part in the mint of the third NFT, the Pilots. It will also be used to buy synergy slots to partipate in system wthat will make you earn more $KDR - Synergy. Aside from that future systems will come out by the end of 2024 mainly around the clan battles game (web2 steam game). You can by CLAN PASSES with $KDR similar to what we see in WF and other systems out there. You can buy physical and digital items. Multiple sinks will be set up for $KDR within the kaidro universe. The team wants $KDR to be used as much as possible. They are designing it so that people have incentive to continue spending $KDR. It was teased that if you use your $KDR to get things from the Kaidro shop you will get SOMETHING in your account in-game (steam). You will be rewarded in other places as well across the IP as well.

A fixed amount of $KDR will be airdropped to NFT holders (sparksuits, spirit guardians, collectible journals and possibly Pilots). Majority of the $KDR will be distributed monthly based on leaderboard placements. To get rank points in the leaderboard you have to play the web3 game on the kaidro.com website. You are required to finish 14 quests and hold NFTs to qualify. I would describe the web3 game as a semi-active form of staking. You combine 3 NFTs to form 1 Synergy. You can get bonuses by combining the same color theme and by using variety of NFTs. You get rank points and $Kalodium as a byproduct of synergy. You can have infinite number of synergy sets. As of this writing, 1 synergy set costs 1 RON and the cost diminshes 50% per synergy slot unlocked till you reach a cap of 0.1 Ronin. You only need to set your synergy once a week. An leadearboard placements will have a monthly snapshot. $KDR will then be distributed monthly.

In addition to synergy, there is a spin wheel where you can spin at a chance for more rank points, as well as other prizes (kinda like moku). To boost your prizes at the spin wheel you can use an NFT as a cartridge. Specific traits will give you boosts in chances depending on what trait is featured that day. $Kalodium can also be used to boost chances. $Kaldoium is a non-tradeable, ERC20 token, that will be used to boost the spinwheel to earn more points.
