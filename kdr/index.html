How does the web3 game work?

I would describe the web3 game as a semi-active form of staking. Kaidro stays away from most web3 game's grind culture and simply rewards you for holding their NFTs. Of course they do require you to "play" or set synergies once per week but that only requires minimal effort. There is also a spin wheel that gives you a chance to win more rank points and prizes. You don't need to grind or play the steam game. This repels people who want to play the game just to milk the IP of it's token. I also like that $KDR is heavily protected from BOTS. The quests require some effort to finish and you need complete 14 quests before you can even participate in $KDR distribution. In addition, synergy requires human effort to setup.

The requirements are as follows: (1) complete 14 quests in the kaidro.com website (This is fairly easy to accomplish) (2) have at least 3 NFTs of the same clan. You will combine 3 NFTs and lock them up to produce synergy. There are bonuses you get when you combine 3 of the same color and when you combine 2 to 3 different NFT types. Base rank points are determined by NFT tier/rarity, the higher the tier the higher the points. NFT parts are used in another mechanic that will boost your chances at prizes in the spin wheel. I made a synergy calculator for you to easily plan your purchases and see what points you're gonna make as well as how much your gonna spend on fees. See the link below.

Synergy slots currently cost 1 $RON and diminishes 50% per additional slot unlocked. There is no limit to the number of Synergy slots you can have on 1 account. We know that RON will be replaced with $KDR once it is launched.

2 things are produced when you engage in synergy (1) Rank points - that acccumulate towards monthly leaderboards AND (2) Kalodium, a non-tradeable, token used to in the spin wheel system. You get 1 Kalodium per 5-10 rank points you make (don't worry it's in the calculator). 1 Kalodium is used per spin. Each spin will give you a chance to win different tiers of prizes, one of them being rank points. The spin wheel prevents the game from being too predictable and gives people of the same number of NFTs a sense of competition. Every month a snapshot will be taken and $KDR will be distributed in proportion to your accumulated rank points and the number of people who played that month.

We know that journal holders can participate in the KDR airdrop if they engage enough with the entire IP and accumulate at least 150 points. They will have 1 free Kalodium per day to spin the wheel. I predict that their share is minimal compared to NFT holders which will get the lion's share.

REMEMBER: Synergy points are integrated into the rank points and KALO is distributed every 0:00 UTC, Saturday. Make sure you sync our official google calendar with yours so you won't miss it!

Spin Wheel

The Spin wheel is a gacha-like game that allows you to spin a wheel to get more rank points. 1 Kalodium will be used per spin. There are other prizes to be won there as well (merchandise, physical comic books, NFTs, whitelists and many more). Prizes are tiered and access to these prizes increases with rarity of NFT parts (NFT cartridge system). No free spins. Unlimited number of spins per day as long as you have Kalodium to burn. Most Kalodium is produced thru synergy and you can get 1 Kalodium daily from logging in. The leaderboard scores take around 24 hours to update so you may not see your points immediately.

On top of your weekly KALO allocation, you will be given 1 KALO per 5 rank point of your final monthly points. Example: By the last day of synergy you have 500 KALO left in your wallet. You were able to accumulate 10,000 rank points that month. You will be given 4000 KALO on top of your 500 KALO lefft in your wallet. By the end of the month you will have 4500 KALO to start the next month.
